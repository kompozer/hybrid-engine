Function Namespace hybridSpritePrototype

Prototype Objects are loaded before the game starts and serve as templates to 'generate' active sprites from events during gameplay. They exists for the lifetime of a level.
Defined in: </home/nils/repository/hybridjs/src/hybridSprite.js>.
